Holly Johanna

Visual Designer

Typography

These typographical works are created digitally in Procreate, and are an excellent indication of my current ability. There is no rhyme or reason to the works - I just feel connected to them.

"Kia Ora" is important to me because of my Kiwi heritage, and Holly is (of course) my name. The final piece is a rework of a hand-drawn piece I did, and is one of my early digital works.

These works are some of the hand-drawn works I have completed over several years.

This shows a progression of my style as I have been trying to extend myself, with the first ("Galaxy") being a recent work, and the last ("Blackfish") being completed in my final year of high school.
