The Girls

The Girls began as an exercise in character development initially designed to represent individuality within a group. They soon evolved into a line of unique characters all representing different personalities.

They were created using ink fine-liners on paper, some were transferred to graphic art using Adobe Illustrator and Procreate to expand my style and skills within this set of artworks. Each girl was a learning exercise in how to convey emotion and personality through a still image, understanding facial features, and using lines to convey light and shadow.
