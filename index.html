My name is Holly. I am a Visual and Graphic designer based in Brighton, UK.
I am a fledgling in the industry having worked for 2 years.
I studied for 1 year at Victoria University in Wellington, NZ (my home town).

Most of what I do is creating pretty things in Illustrator, Photoshop, or in Procreate. Some of my past work has been creating learning resources for online tests, as well as various bits and pieces for small businesses.

I dabble in websites, and have a working knowledge of HTML and CSS.
This website is some of my work!
